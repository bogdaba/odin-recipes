Wodzionka

Description

Very basic soup of Silesian kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Stale bread
  • Grounded garlic
  • Onion
  • Animal fat

Steps

  1. Boil some water
  2. Cut the bread and add to a bowl
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients
  4. Pour the boiling water over
  5. Notify your neighbours about ready dinner with a window pipe[1]
