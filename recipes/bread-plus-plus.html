Smørrebrød

Description

Very basic Scandinavian meal. Literally butter and bread.

Ingredients

  • Bread (not stale)
  • Butter
  • Toppings (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the bread if not cut already
  2. Spread the butter on bread slices (it is preferrable to use only one side of the bread slice)
  3. Add toppings
